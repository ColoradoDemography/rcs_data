2020 Residential Construction Data Request

Overview:

The State Demography Office creates sub-county population estimates that are used for several programs in the state. The first phase of this process involves estimates of total housing units. These data will be used as the foundation for your entity's population estimate.

  • We are requesting monthly counts on new housing units only.
    • We do not need an accounting of permits issued for building improvements or for new non-residential structures (e.g., barns and other out buildings).
    • For new single family housing units, please count each one singly.
    • For new apartments and multi-unit buildings, please report the number of individual units, not the number of permits.
  • If your jurisdiction does not use building permits, certificates of occupancy, or demolition permits, please contact us about alternative data via email.


Instructions:
  • Review the data for your area(s) below.
  • Complete the spreadsheet that was emailed to you. You can also find the spreadsheet below.
  • Make sure to enter the name of your local government, and your contact information.
  • Multi-county places should complete one spreadsheet for each county their municipality is located in.
  • If you find the data on our website is incorrect, including zeros, or missing previous years, please send us updated information and use RED TEXT so we know it has changed.
  • Please send us data through the most current month you have available.
  • Return your spreadsheet(s) to adam.bickford@state.co.us or nancy.gedeon@state.co.us by the due date (depending on who sent you the request).

Due Date: December 15, 2020
Definitions:
New Residential Building Permits
  • Number of monthly building permits that result in a new residential unit being created.
  • For new apartments and multi-unit buildings, please report the number of individual units, not the number of permits.
Certificates of Occupancy
  • Monthly number of certificates of occupancy that results in a new residential unit being finished.
Demolition Permits
  • Demolition permits that would lead to the loss of a residential unit. These would not include joint demolition and building permits that allow demolition and rebuilding on one permit.
  • Monthly data of number of units permitted for demolition.
Mobile Homes or Manufactured Housing Units that did not receive building permits
  • Mobile Homes or Manufactured Housing units are measured with a count of units placed.
  • Semi-annual Mobile Homes or Manufactured Housing Units counts, January 1 to June 30 and July 1 to December 31.

Data Type Time Period for Vintage 2020
Building Permits January 1, 2019 to December 31, 2019
Certificates of Occupancy July 1, 2019 to June 30, 2020
Demolition Permits July 1, 2019 to June 30, 2020
Mobile Homes or Manufactured Housing Units that did not receive building permits July 1, 2019 to June 30, 2020 (Half year)
